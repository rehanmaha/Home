Travel

My second favourite hobby is a difficult thing to do since it involves money, and I barely have any money in my bank account. However, since my parents have more money than me, they were somewhat able to help me fulfill my hobby. Travelling a lot is my number one in my "Things to do before I die" list. I have travelled a bit already, but I would like to do more. Here are the few places I have lived so far.

Pakistan

  1. Karachi
  2. Lilla, Jhelum
  3. Islamabad

I was born in Karachi, Pakistan. However, I never got the chance to experience the lifestyle since I moved six months after my birth. However, I visited Pakistan a few years back and went to Islamabad and my grandmother's village. Pakistan is a rich country, both in culture and people. Britannaica has more in-depth explanation of Pakistan if you want to research more.

Source: Google Maps

Qatar

  1. Doha
  2. Al Wakrah
  3. Umm Sa'id
  4. Al Khawr
  5. Ar Ru'ays
  6. Dukhan

Qatar is a small country, but it's had the most impact on me. Although I don't live there anymore, I visit every few years to meet my cousins. Each time I return, I miss Qatar; its people, its architectures and mostly, my family. This country is beautiful in every aspect and I hope to live there in the future. Since it's so small, I have visited most of the cities.

Source: Google Maps

Oman

  1. Sohar
  2. Muscat

After moving from Qatar, I lived in Sohar, Oman for three years. Around this time, I was going through my “childhood” years, therefore, growing up in Oman has heavily influenced my likes and dislikes. Oman’s history is really rich, and the forts there are my most favourite memories. They made me have an interest towards architecture, especially Islamic Architecture. Oman is another place I would like to live in the future.

Source: Google Maps

Canada

  1. London
  2. Toronto

As of now, I have lived in Canada for about 7 years. Although it is my least favourite country out of all I’ve lived in, I can’t deny the privilege of living here. The education was the main cause my parents decided to leave the previous luxurious and free-lived countries. I’m very grateful for the opportunities I have gotten and I am glad to have grown up here.

Source: Google Maps
