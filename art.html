Art

My favourite hobby is doing art. Surprisingly, it also something I do once in months. However, whenever I do it, I feel content. Although my pieces might not be the best, I am always progressing in my skills each time I do art. Just like any other skill, precision takes practice. The four pieces below were all completed this past year and this year. Hope you like them!

All images on this page source: Me

Hagia Sophia

I did this art piece after I binge-watched "Rise of Empires: Ottoman." The show was one of the best shows I have watched, and the cinematography was outstanding. The primarly building shown in the show was Hagia Sophia. The show portrayed it as something magical and intensely desirable. Therefore, while I was obsessed with the show, I decided to put my short-lasted obsession into a piece of architectural work.

Untitled piece by Abdur Rahman Chughtai

After a three-year break from drawing people, I felt like trying again a few weeks ago. This art piece is a distinctive painting style influenced by Mughal art. The painter of this piece is Abdur Rahman Chughtai, a renowned and intellectual artist from Pakistan. Mughul-era paintings have been a massive interest of mine, and hopefully, I will recreate one in the future. This piece makes me feel nice because the women are wearing cultural clothing, shalwar kameez, and I like that.
